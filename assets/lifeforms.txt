Name: Protocellus Primordia
Taxonomy: Domain: Protozoa; Kingdom: Protista
Description: Protocellus Primordia thrives in high-temperature, mineral-rich water, absorbing nutrients directly from the surrounding environment. It replicates via a rudimentary form of binary fission. Approximately 1-2 micrometers in diameter, these protocells exhibit a simple cell membrane enveloping RNA strands and basic metabolic machinery.
